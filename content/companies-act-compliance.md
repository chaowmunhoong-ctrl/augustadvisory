# Companies Act Compliance Checklist

The Companies Act 2016 sets out specific requirements for Malaysian companies. Here's what you need to know.

## Important Deadlines

- Annual filing with SSM
- Director and shareholder information updates
- Annual general meetings
- Financial statement filing
